Japan's premier
gathering for the
Python community.

PyCon JP is an annual conference bringing together Python developers, researchers, educators, and enthusiasts from across Japan and around the world. Three days of talks, workshops, sprints, and hallway conversations in the beautiful city of Hiroshima.

Whether you write Python for data science, web development, automation, or education — this is your place to learn, share, and connect with people who love the language as much as you do.
